MY CREATIVE FLOW

I am an end-to-end Product Designer specializing in measurable and intuitive digital solutions. My expertise covers the full design spectrum: from user research and branding to wireframing, UX/UI design for web/mobile, and the development of interactive prototypes and scalable design systems.

My core workflow begins with foundational hand-sketching for rapid ideation. Figma serves as the central hub for all UX/UI design, design systems, and interactive prototyping across web and mobile. I use Maze for design validation and testing, and the Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign) for visual asset creation and print collateral.

A successful design is one that effectively meets the needs of the user (validated through usability testing) while simultaneously achieving the defined business objectives (measured by KPIs like conversion rate, task completion, or engagement).

I ensure all designs adhere to WCAG standards for color contrast, typography, and clear visual hierarchy. I prioritize designing with keyboard navigation and screen-reader compatibility in mind from the wireframing stage.

I handle feedback by actively listening, asking clarifying questions, and iterating on designs based on constructive criticism. I view feedback as an essential part of the design process that helps improve the final product.
